Why We Exist

01

A different
kind of
table.

The manuscript consultation industry is broken. Coverage farms charging $300 for notes generated by an algorithm. Contest readers who've never sold a script ranking your work with a number that means nothing. Development "coaches" preying on ambition to charge $500 an hour for a conversation you could have with any working writer who actually gives a damn.

We built The Manuscript Collective because we couldn't find what we needed ourselves. As working writers — in TV, features, publishing, and comics — we know what it actually feels like to have your work stress-tested by someone who respects both the craft and you.

"You don't need a grade. You need a conversation with someone who's been in the room."

Every person who reads your work at The Manuscript Collective is a credentialed professional writer. They'll give you their actual opinion, delivered with the care of a colleague who wants to see you succeed.

Whether you're finishing your first pilot or rewriting your fifth feature, you deserve notes that help you get better, conversation that can open doors, and cheerleaders who believe in your talent. We specialize in all three.

Who We Are

Writers helping writers

We are not just a service. We are a collective of working professional writers who believe in the power of honest, peer-level creative dialogue. Every consultant you work with has a track record: produced scripts, published novels, optioned work, credits. Not credentials from a workshop. Actual work in the world.

We specialize in the formats that matter to you: television drama and comedy, features of every genre, literary and commercial fiction, and the full spectrum of comics and graphic narrative.

More importantly, we specialize in the conversation. We'll tell you what's working. We'll tell you what isn't. We'll do it with honesty, warmth, and zero agenda beyond helping you grow.
